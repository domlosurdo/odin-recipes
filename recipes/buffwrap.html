Buffalo Chicken Wrap

Enjoy a spicy Buffalo Chicken Wrap, perfect for a quick lunch or snack. Easy to make and packed with flavor, it's a delicious handheld meal that brings the bold taste of Buffalo wings to your table!

Ingredients

  • Shredded or fried chicken
  • Burrito-size Tortilla
  • Buffalo Sauce
  • Ranch or Blue cheese dressing
  • shredded cheese
  • shredded lettuce
  • diced tomatoes
  • chopped onions

Steps

  1. Prepare all ingredients as desired.
  2. Toss chicken in buffalo sauce.
  3. Lay tortilla on counter and apply some dressing as a base.
  4. Add your chicken first, followed by the other ingredients.
  5. If you love sauce, add a bit more buffalo sauce or dressing.
  6. Use the far end of the wrap to press ingredients to your side of the Tortilla.
  7. Fold the sides of the wrap in, and roll away from you.
  8. (Hint: apply decent pressure to ensure a nicely packed wrap.)
  9. Cut in half if desired, and Enjoy!
